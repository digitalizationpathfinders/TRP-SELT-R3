Before you begin

The CRA may grant taxpayer relief when an individual or business experience circumstances beyond their control that prevented them from meeting their tax obligations.

Use this tool to find out if your situation may qualify for relief of penalties and/or interest and to help prepare you to fill out your taxpayer relief request.

Note: This tool does not save any data if you navigate away from this page.
